🌬️ Nadi Shodhana: Alternate Nostril Breathing

This ancient pranayama technique balances the left and right hemispheres of the brain, harmonizes your nervous system, and creates the perfect state for quantum manifestation. I learned this powerful practice from traditional yoga teachings, and it amplifies the effectiveness of affirmations tremendously.

How to Practice:

  1. Mudra: Use your right hand - fold the index and middle fingers down, use thumb and ring finger
  2. Close right nostril with your thumb, inhale slowly through left nostril for 4 counts
  3. Close both nostrils briefly, then release thumb and close left nostril with ring finger
  4. Exhale through right nostril for 4 counts
  5. Inhale through right nostril for 4 counts
  6. Close both nostrils briefly, then release ring finger and close right nostril with thumb
  7. Exhale through left nostril for 4 counts
  8. This completes one round. Practice 5-10 rounds before your affirmations

Benefits for Quantum Work: This breathing technique activates the parasympathetic nervous system, balances ida and pingala nadis (energy channels), and creates coherence between heart and brain - the perfect state for programming your reality through affirmations.
